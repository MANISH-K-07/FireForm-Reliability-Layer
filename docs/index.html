🔥 FireForm Reliability Middleware

A safety-preserving middleware layer for structured emergency incident extraction.

Overview

FireForm's incident intake pipeline relies on structured outputs generated by Large Language Models (LLMs). Raw LLM extraction often produces missing or invalid fields. This middleware ensures structural determinism, logical consistency, and safe recoverability without fabricating data.

Prototype Highlights

Even when raw extraction fails 97.33% of the time:

  • 41.33% of reports become fully structured and valid
  • 86.15% of salvageable reports are safely repaired
  • Unsafe guess count remains 0

Safety is prioritized over aggressive auto-completion.
